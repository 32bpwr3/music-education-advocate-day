### Hunter O'Brien to perform at the State Capitol

Best of luck to Hunter O'Brien on his performance inside the State Capitol on Wednesday, March 9 during 2016 Music Education Advocacy Day. The Concert will take place on the Glass Floor Rotunda (1st floor) starting at 12 p.m. Hunter will perform in the 2nd group.
